* About Wanderer

Wanderer is an old text-based game originally written By Steven Shipway in c on on a sun/3-160

** Gameplay
In the following description, I will show the character used for each item when played in full-screen mode (by pressing '#').  This is also the character used in the screen files that run the game.
In wanderer, you are a spider (@) who must gather all the treasure (*) in a level and then go to the exit (X), all before the alloted number of moves expire.  The game is played over many levels, each becoming available as you finish the prior level.  When you finish a level, you are also provided with a password which can be used to jump directly to that level in a subsequent playing session.
In levels you will encounter the following:
- Treasure (*). Collect this to get 10 points.  You must collect them all to be able to exit and finish the level
- Exit (X).  Go here once you collect all the treasure (including capturing Baby Monsters).  You get 250 points for successfully using the exit.
- Passable earth (:).  You can dig through this, clearing it away.  You get 1 point for each
- Solid rock (#) which cannot be blown up
- Solid rock (=), or (\_) which _can_ be blown up
- A Teleport (T) which takes you to a new location (the new location is unknown in gameplay until you take the teleport, but in the screen files is denoted by 'A').  Using the teleport is optional, but you get a 50 point bonus if you do use it.
- Boulder (O).  Falls down.  Other boulders and arrows fall/slip around it.  A boulder falling on you kills you.  Boulders fall around other boulders unless both spaces around the boulder are blocked (potentially by other boulders).  You can push a single boulder sideways, but not multiple boulders next to each other.
- Left Arrow (<).  Shoots to the left.
- Right Arrow (>).  Shoots to the right.  Arrows can begin a level suspended and ready to shoot, but if you come near, they trigger and shoot.  Arrows shooting into you kills you.  These slip around boulders and slopes, but not other things (including other arrows).  You can push and arrow up or down, but not multiple arrows in a vertical line.
- Landmine (!).  If you touch it, you die.
- Baby Monster (S). Baby monsters travel around surfaces that they touch.  When a baby monster hits a cage it is captured and you get 50 points. The cage also becomes a diamond.  If a Baby Monster is on a screen, it must be captured in the cage and the resulting diamond collected before you can exit the level.
- Baby Monster Cage (+).  Traps Baby Monsters if they touch it and turns into a treasure when they do.
- Slopes (/) and ('\' in gameplay, but '\\' in screen files).  Things like boulders, arrows and balloons slide off these when they encounter them.
- Time Capsule (C). Touching this gives you 5 points and 250 extra moves with which to finish the level.
- Big Monster (M).  Chases you and kills you if it catches you.  You can kill it (and collect a 100 point award) by arranging to have it hit with an arrow or having a boulder fall on top of it.
- Bomb (B).  Explodes and destroys the area around it if hit with a boulder or arrow.  If you are in the blast radius, you die.
- Thingy (~).  Something you can push around and it stays where you put it.  Boulder and arrows do not slip around it.
- Open Space (<space> or '-').  You can move around freely in this.
- Wall (|)


** Commands
- Movement: either arrow keys or 'hjkl' (for left, down, up, right, respectively)
- Toggle Full-Screen: #
- Jump to Level (using password): ~
- Save Game: S
- Restore Game: R
- Help: ?
- Show Map: !
- Center Map: @
- Quit: q
- Exit: x
